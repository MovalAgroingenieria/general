=============
Album Gallery
=============

Organize photos and videos in albums.

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:

Description
===========

This module introduces a gallery for your webpage so that you can keep your
photos and videos organized within albums, and display them on your website
in whatever order you want.
